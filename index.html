Mingxuan Cai

I am a third-year Ph.D. student at Berkeley EECS Department, advised by Prof. Laura Waller. Previously, I had the privilege to work with Prof. Cuifang Kuang at Zhejiang University.

My recent research focuses on Computational Imaging, with applications in space-time imaging, event-based vision, and computational fluorescence imaging.
